Three Part Thesis

A strong thesis moves like a river. The headwaters gather the inarguable facts — the common ground every reader stands on upstream. The main channel is where the current commits to a single, forceful direction. The delta is where the argument opens back out, touching multiple shores, entering waters larger than itself.

Story 1
The Headwaters
What can every reader agree on before you make your case? The inarguable context your argument builds from.
Story 2
The Main Channel
Where does the current commit? Your single, arguable claim — the one direction only your argument flows.
Story 3
The Delta's Spread
What larger shores does your argument reach? The conversations, stakes, and worlds it enters beyond your immediate topic.
